Welcome to our Pokemon Dashboard!

We are a team of developers excited to share the world of Pokemon!

Our story begins in 1995, when the franchise called Pokemon, formerly known as "Pocket Monsters", started in Japan. Pokemon are small creatures you can catch and train to fight each other in the Pokemon Universe. For those who are still confused, think of a wrestling match! Trainers work with fighters who then wrestle to win. As you toggle through the website pages, you will learn all about the individual strengths about each Pokemon, to help you make an informed decision as a new player.

We want you to be as excited about Pokemon as we are, and are eager to share our knowledge of the Pokemon Universe!
